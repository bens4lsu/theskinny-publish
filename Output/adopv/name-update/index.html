Name/Update

April 13, 2007

The votes are in. I put a poll about what to name the kid up on the main page, and here are the results.

Name The Kid

We did this once before in the early days of theskinnyonbenny, but with a cat. The boy's name in Russia is Ivan Konstantinovich _______. You help chose what his American name will be. Actually, your vote is in no way binding, but it might be fun.
