Home Page for the Pegasus Velvet Elvis

The current Velvet Elvis is a Pegasus 50, manufactured by Pegasus Yachts, Kranj, Slovenia. She is hull #10, and the first Pegasus to be imported into the United States.

She currently lives at the New Orleans municipal marina, and she has her own website.
