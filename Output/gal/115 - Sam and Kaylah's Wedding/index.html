Sam and Kaylah Wedding Pics

September 26 - 27, 2014

These are my pictures from the weekend of Sam and Kaylah's wedding. Because they had a real photographer there, I have precious few photos of the bride and groom — in fact there are as many photos here of the two of them together as there are of Elvis Presley and Johnny Depp standing together — but there are some good ones of the setup and rehearsal that don't seem to be included in the official photo albums. Mostly, though, these are cute pics of well-dressed kids.

On top of that, I added a few of my favorites from the real photographer's set in here too, so don't be confused if you've seen one or two of these before.
