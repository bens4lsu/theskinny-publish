Russia Trip 1 - Yaroslavl

March 12-15, 2007

We spent the first part of our trip in Yaroslavl. Yaroslavl is a 4 to 5 hour drive or train ride northeast from Moscow. The orphanage that we visited is in this town, and most of our time there was dealing with things that we had to take care of for that process. Meeting our son for the first time was incredibly fun. Other than that, there are just pictures from us walking around town.
