Holiday Collection 2006-2007

January 2007

This is a series of photos from a handful of nights during the Christmas/New Years season at the turn of 2006-07. These are really great shots, and I can take credit for almost none of them. Most of the early pictures are from a night out in Natchitoches. The narritive for that one is here.
