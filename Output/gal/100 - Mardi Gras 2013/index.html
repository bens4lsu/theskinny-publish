2013 Mardi Gras Parades

February 7 - 12, 2013

This one starts with a few pictures from the night of Muses, and then a few from Sunday before Mardi Gras (is that Fat Sunday? Wouldn't know the French for it.) Most of the pictures are from Tuesday, where we walked around street parades and parties in the Quarter and in the Marigny. On our way out, we saw a small group of Indians, quickly parked the car, and followed them to where a lot of Indians met up to dance and chant. It's definitely an amazing sight, and if you skip everything else, you should skip down and check out those pictures.
