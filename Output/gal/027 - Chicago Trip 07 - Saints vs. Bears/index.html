Chicago

January 21, 2007

On January 21, 2007 the New Orleans Saints visited Soldier Field in Chicago for the NFC Championship game against the Chicago Bears. Although we would have liked to see a better performance by the Saints, a December NFL game in a snowy stadium up north is something that everyone should do at least once. This is a collection of almost every semi-decent photograph that I took on game day.
