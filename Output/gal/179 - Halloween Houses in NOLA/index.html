Halloween Houses in NOLA

October 22, 2023

Almost every year, we go to New Orleans for a weekend or two in October. One of the most entertaining things to do is to see some of the Halloween decorations. This year, we stopped to take pictures at the skeleton house uptown, and at Ghost Manor in Irish Channel. Ghost Manor actually should be seen at night, as it's mostly light-show and animatronics that aren't on during the day, but we were there in the day this year, so that's what I have for pictures.

For the skeletons, get you a big screen so you can read the labels.
