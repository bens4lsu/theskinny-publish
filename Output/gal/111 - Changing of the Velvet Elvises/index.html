Changing of the Velvet Elvises

April 7-22, 2014

It's official. Our 16 year old Rhodes 22 Velvet Elvis has been traded in. It was tough to let her go. There were so many great times aboard. Our new boat, also named Velvet Elvis, is an Oceanis 41, made by Beneteau. We picked her up this month for a four-day Easter shakedown before returning her to the boatyard for some finish work.

Boat nerds might enjoy the shots at the end of the boat being lifted out of the water and driven away.
