Behind the Scenes at Audubon Zoo

April 14-15, 2012

Several times each year, the Audubon Zoo in New Orleans has a Zoo After Dark program. You come in and pitch a tent after the zoo closes, and then you see animals and have activities through the night. In the morning, you get breakfast, followed by a guided walk through the zoo before it opens. We went for the behind the scenes swamp tour in April.
