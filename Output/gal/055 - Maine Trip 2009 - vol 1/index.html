Maine Trip 2009, Vol. 1

July 30, 2009 - August 4, 2009

We went up for a week in Maine, but for the first 24 hours, we're still in Boston. We went up to Maine on Friday evening, with our friends Josh and Lisa. They generously put us up in their cabin in the woods, near Moose Pond. Even more generously, they added Crystal, Brent, and their three kids to the mix on Sunday. These photos get us from the airport on Thursday, July 30, to the end of the day on Tuesday, August 4. Another gallery will finish out the week.
