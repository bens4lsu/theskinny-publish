Russia Trip 1 - Moscow

March 16-17, 2007

On March 16, we must have walked fifteen miles, covering block after block in order to see as much as we could in the short amount of free time that we had in the city. On the 17th, we did a very small amount of shopping, and then went to an Irish pub for St. Patrick's Day. This is a big collection and the early pictures are very much about the archetecture of Moscow.
