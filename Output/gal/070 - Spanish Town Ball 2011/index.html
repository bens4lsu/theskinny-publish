2011 Spanish Town Ball

February 12, 2011

The theme this year was to parody the oil spill in some fashion. Jim built oil platform table centerpieces, and he did them so that the above-water parts were way above our head. That left us to come up with costumes as various types of oil-stained sea life.

As you've surely seen by this point, Sharples and I were memaids. The costumes were much more popular than I would have imagined. I must have taken 50 pictures with strangers.
