Overnight at the Zoo, Then the Boat Festival

October 11 - 12, 2014

We went to our second Safari After Dark at the Audubon Zoo this October. (The first time we went was back in 2012. This was a little better organized and the exhibit was as fun as anything you can imagine. The only downer was when I tried to scare a little girl who I don't even know, sold it a little too convincingly, and spilled my grown up drink all over my shirt during the night hike.

The next day, we went to the Wooden Boat Festival in Madisonville on the way home.
