Northern Leeward Islands

May 26 - June 9

Guadelopue, Saba, Statia, and Sint Maarten should be the title. But then there was also a day trip to Saint Martin, which is not the same as Sint Maarten but which is on the same island. And I only just remembered that Montserrat is in there too.

So this gallery covers a lot of ground. All really unique islands that deserve more time. Here are some links to things I wrote: Guadeloupe, Montserrat, Saba, Statia, and Sint Maarten.

As you can guess, visiting 6 countries in two weeks isn't adequate, and in some cases, I didn't come away with a whole lot of pictures.
