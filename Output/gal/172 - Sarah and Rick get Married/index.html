Sarah and Rick Get Married

October 2, 2021

A few pictures from a single day, when my sister got married. It started as a rainy Saturday, and the only thing to do in Bay St Louis was to sit at a bar and drink beer. That left me a little bit loaded for the main event, but at least by then the weather had cleared. It's a shame that I didn't have the motor coordination to pin on my mother's corsage, but overall, it was a very very fun weekend.
